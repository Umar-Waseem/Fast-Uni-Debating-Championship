About Fast Debating Society

FAST Debating Society was founded back in 2005 by Samir Anwar Butt and Zubair Nabi, and since then it has become one of the most prestigious societies at FAST University. Here at FAST Debating Society we motivate people to have the courage to speak. It’s not just a debating society, it’s a platform for anyone who wants to boost their self-confidence and learn the art of persuasion and vocalisation. We do not reject anyone based on their debating/speaking skills. Instead, we try to motivate and teach each and every individual who comes at our door with the aim of self-improvement.

About Fast

The National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences holds the honour of being Pakistan’s first multi-campus University chartered by the Federal Government in the private sector. The University started with an impressive advantage in that it inherited five first rate campuses located in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar and Faisalabad in spacious buildings and prestigious settings. Over more than two decades, FAST has become the hallmark of excellence both locally and internationally. The campus offers state of the art facilities in professional, academic, and extracurricular terms.
